Hawaiian (Ham & Pineapple)

If you think you don’t like Hawaiian pizza, that’s because you haven’t tried homemade! I used to be one of “those people”…😂

Ingredients

  • Pizza sauce
  • mozzarella
  • ham
  • pineapple

Steps

  1. Spread pizza sauce over base, followed by cheese.
  2. Scatter remaining ingredients over pizza, and bake.
